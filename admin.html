Slaughterhouse Memorial...

Slaughterhouse Memorial is built in Dhaka's Rayer Bazar Itkhola. It was built in 1971 at the end of Bangladesh's War of Liberation as a tribute to all the best intellectuals and others killed by the Pakistan Army with the help of their allies. The memorial was erected at the site of the massacre. Among the slain intellectuals were academics, doctors, journalists, writers, film directors and other professionals.
